Coordinate Frames and Transformations

Master the mathematical foundations of robotics by learning how to represent positions, orientations, and transformations in 3D space using coordinate frames.

Learning Objectives

Understand what a coordinate frame is and why it is used in robotics.

Learn how to represent points and vectors in different coordinate frames.

Apply translation and rotation transformations between frames.

Use simulation tools (GeoGebra 3D) to visualize frame interactions.

Analyze sensor and robot motion data using frame transformations.
